# The Local House - Elegant Design System

## Design Philosophy
**Classico, Sofisticato, Timeless** - Un sito che rispecchia l'eleganza di un boutique hotel di lusso con radici nel design Art Deco di Miami.

---

## Color Palette (Refined)

### Primary Colors
- **Deep Navy**: #1A3A52 (header, accents)
- **Warm Gold**: #D4AF37 (accents, highlights)
- **Cream**: #F5F1E8 (background, text light)
- **Charcoal**: #2C2C2C (text primary)

### Secondary Colors
- **Soft Coral**: #E8B4A8 (subtle accents)
- **Seafoam**: #A8D8D8 (secondary accents)
- **Light Gold**: #E8D5B7 (backgrounds)

---

## Typography

### Display Font
**Playfair Display** (Google Fonts)
- Headlines, titles
- Weight: 700, 600
- Letter spacing: 0.5px
- Elegant, timeless, luxury feel

### Body Font
**Lora** (Google Fonts)
- Body text, descriptions
- Weight: 400, 500
- Line height: 1.7
- Readable, sophisticated

### Accent Font
**Montserrat** (Google Fonts)
- CTAs, labels, navigation
- Weight: 500, 600
- Letter spacing: 0.5px

---

## Spacing System
- **Base unit**: 8px
- **Sections**: 80px (desktop), 40px (mobile)
- **Components**: 16px, 24px, 32px
- **Generous whitespace** - Luxury feel

---

## Typography Scale

| Level | Font | Size | Weight | Usage |
|-------|------|------|--------|-------|
| **H1** | Playfair Display | 48px | 700 | Page titles |
| **H2** | Playfair Display | 36px | 700 | Section titles |
| **H3** | Playfair Display | 28px | 600 | Subsections |
| **H4** | Lora | 24px | 500 | Card titles |
| **Body** | Lora | 16px | 400 | Main text |
| **Small** | Montserrat | 14px | 500 | Labels, captions |

---

## Visual Elements

### Borders & Dividers
- Thin gold lines (#D4AF37) for elegance
- Soft shadows: `0 2px 8px rgba(0,0,0,0.08)`
- No harsh borders

### Buttons
- **Primary**: Deep Navy background, Cream text
- **Secondary**: Outline style with Gold border
- **Hover**: Subtle gold underline or background shift
- Padding: 12px 32px
- Border radius: 2px (minimal, elegant)

### Cards
- Cream background (#F5F1E8)
- Subtle shadow
- Gold accent border (top or left)
- Generous padding: 32px

---

## Layout Principles

1. **Asymmetric but balanced** - Not grid-based, more organic
2. **Generous margins** - 40-80px on sides
3. **Image-text interplay** - Images and text alternate
4. **Vertical rhythm** - Consistent spacing between sections
5. **Whitespace is content** - Don't fill every pixel

---

## Component Styles

### Hero Section
- Full-width image (high quality)
- Overlay with semi-transparent navy (0.3 opacity)
- Large Playfair Display text centered
- Minimal CTA buttons below

### Feature Cards
- 3-column grid (desktop)
- Cream background with gold top border
- Icon + title + description
- Hover: subtle lift effect

### Testimonials
- Quote mark (gold)
- Lora italic text
- Author name in Montserrat
- Star rating in gold

### CTA Sections
- Navy background
- Cream text
- Centered, generous padding
- Single clear button

---

## Imagery Style

- **Quality**: Professional photography only
- **Tone**: Warm, inviting, not sterile
- **Aspect ratios**: Mix of 16:9, 4:3, square
- **Filters**: Subtle warm tone (no heavy filters)
- **Composition**: Lifestyle shots, not just product shots

---

## Navigation

- **Header**: Sticky, minimal (logo + nav items)
- **Logo**: Elegant, simple, gold accent
- **Nav items**: Montserrat, 14px, spaced out
- **Active state**: Gold underline
- **Mobile**: Hamburger menu, full-screen overlay

---

## Animations

- **Transitions**: 300ms ease-in-out
- **Hover effects**: Subtle color shift or underline
- **Scroll animations**: Fade-in on scroll (not aggressive)
- **No autoplay**: User controls all motion

---

## Accessibility

- **Contrast**: WCAG AA compliant
- **Font sizes**: Minimum 16px for body
- **Line height**: 1.6+ for readability
- **Focus states**: Clear, visible
- **Alt text**: All images described

---

## Responsive Design

- **Desktop**: 1200px+
- **Tablet**: 768px - 1199px
- **Mobile**: < 768px
- **Breakpoints**: Fluid, not rigid
- **Touch targets**: 44px minimum
