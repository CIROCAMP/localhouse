## Brunch Planning Tips

**Make Reservations:** Popular spots fill up quickly, especially on weekends. Book ahead through OpenTable or directly with the restaurant.

**Arrive Early:** If you prefer not to wait, arrive before 11AM. You'll have shorter waits and a more relaxed experience.

**Try the Signature Dishes:** Each restaurant has signature brunch items. Don't be afraid to order what they're known for—it's usually worth it.

**Explore Different Neighborhoods:** While South Beach is the most famous, SoFi (South of Fifth) and the Art Deco District offer excellent brunch options with less crowding.

**Check Hours:** Some restaurants only serve brunch on weekends. Confirm hours before planning your visit.
