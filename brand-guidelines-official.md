# The Local House - Official Brand Guidelines

## Brand Voice
**"Easy Going, Friendly, Welcoming"**

Step away from Miami Beach's spirited scene and discover our laid-back hideaway nestled off the strip. Ours is a place to soak in the present amid a warm and welcoming vibe where locals frequent and visitors getaway. Evoking that small-town feeling, we stand for everything that sun-kissed coastal living should be.

## Brand Textures
1. **Blue Marble** - Teal/turquoise marbled texture
2. **Stucco** - Warm peach/sand texture
3. **Textured Paper** - White/cream paper texture

---

## Brand Elements
- **Watercolor brush strokes** in brand colors (Sky, Coral, Sunshine)
- Soft, organic shapes
- Layered visual elements

---

## Imagery Style
- **Mid-saturated**, warm and muted look
- High resolution images of:
  - Dishes and cocktails
  - Property and lifestyle
  - People enjoying outdoor activities
  - Social interactions
- Filter: VSCO Cam A4 +8.8
- Goal: Showcase "local's paradise" feeling

---

## Key Messaging
- "Everyone's a local here"
- "#EATLIKEALOCAL"
- "Mouthfuls of vibrant, beachside flavors"
- "The oceanside retreat you've been yearning for"

## Logo
- Two components: Primary wordmark + city location (MIAMI)
- Minimum clearspace: 33px on all sides
- Minimum size: 0.75" print / 120px digital
